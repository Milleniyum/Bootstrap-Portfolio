About Me

Born and raised in Syracuse, NY, I moved to Florida in March, 2002. I began working at Walt Disney World two months later and have held many different roles over my 16 years with the company. Those roles include Call Center Operator, Training Specialist, Vacation Advisor, Administrative Assistant and Software Developer.

I'm an avid reader, roller coaster rider, World of Warcraft player, movie-goer, pop funko collector, fish watcher and dog/cat petter. And, of course, I enjoy programming!
